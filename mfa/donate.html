Stories

Meet a few of the animals who's lives were saved by Mercy For Animals

Meet Roy

Roy was one of four calves rescued by MFA after an investigation of the E6 Cattle Conmpany in Texas. Our investigator found workers bashing in the heads of calves with pickaxes and hammers, dragging calves like Roy around by the ears, and leaving others to slowly die from illness and injury. Thanks to MFA felony cruelty charges were filed against farm workers, and Roy and his brothers are now living the good life in the pastrue of an animal sanctuary.
