Food for Thought

Pigs, cows, chickens, fish, and other farmed animals are smart and unique individuals just like the dogs and cats we share our homes with. But ehind the closed doors of modern farms they endure brutal cruelty. Because animal cruelty laws do not protect them, farmed animals are often beaten, mutilated, and painfully slaughtered.
